## 3. Why the gap exists (mechanism diagnosis)

- **Label-protocol audit** — SWAN-SF labels vs our HEK labeler on the same windows: agreement 0.9981, positive-class Jaccard 0.9315 (confusion: {'both_positive': 1170, 'swansf_only_positive': 84, 'hek_only_positive': 2, 'both_negative': 43733}).
- **Distribution shift** — median feature KS 0.055; most shifted: MEANSHR__max (KS 0.128).
- **Model divergence** — Spearman rank correlation between the two models' feature importances: 0.158. rank correlation near 1 = both models learned the same physics; low correlation = at least one model leans on dataset-specific shortcuts that do not transfer.
